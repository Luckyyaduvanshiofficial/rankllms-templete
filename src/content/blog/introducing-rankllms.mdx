For the past year, keeping track of which AI model is best for a specific use case has become impossible. Every week a new frontier model drops, and developers are left scrambling to check its context window, API cost, and performance on the SWE-bench.

Today, we're solving that.

## Welcome to RankLLMs

RankLLMs is a continuously updated directory of over 300 Large Language Models. Instead of relying on vibes, we track strict benchmarks.

### Finding the Best Coding Model

When deciding on a coding model, HumanEval and SWE-bench are the gold standard. For example, here's a live look at OpenAI's latest flagship model right from our database:

### Finding Cost-Effective Models

You don't always need a massive frontier model. Sometimes you just need to parse basic JSON or do simple RAG. That's when open-weight models like Llama 3 8B or Mistral come in handy.

## What's Next?
In the coming weeks, we will be launching:
1. **Interactive Comparisons:** Check two models side-by-side. 
2. **Community Reviews:** Leave your own ratings based on subjective use-cases (e.g. "Best for Creative Writing").
3. **Verified Benchmarking:** Integration with automated eval suites to update the database the minute new scores drop.

Stay tuned, and happy building!
